E-Z Drop Biscuits

Provided by Angela

Description

A set of biscuits with a bit of cowboy flare; no kneading or layering required. Just mix, drop, and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 1 cup milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C)
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt
  3. Stir in butter and milk until batter is moistened (and no more)
  4. Drop batter onto a lightly greased pan
  5. Bake in preheated oven until golden on the edges. About 10-12 min
  6. Let cool, then serve!
