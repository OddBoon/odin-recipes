Chicken Alfredo

  • 1 chicken breast
  • <1oz of olive oil (or any prefered greaser)
  • 1oz of lemon juice(optional)
  • 12oz of Alfredo Sauce(either a jar or the recipe foundhere)
  • 16oz [1 Box of] Fettuccine

Directions

  1. Bring the stovetop to med to high heat and bring a pot of water to a rolling boil
  2. Bring out the chicken breast and dice it into 1/4" cubes.
  3. Bring another pan to medium heat and drizzle your oil onto it. Put your diced chicken into the pan. Sprinkle your salt/pepper to taste.
  4. Cook for 5-6 minutes, or until the edges are turning golden brown. If your wanting to include lemon juice, I would add it before the chicken is browned, but isn't pink, about 2-3 minutes in.
  5. Once the pasta is al dante, strain it out with a Colander. Put it back into the pot.
  6. If you handmade the sauce, toss it into the pot at this time, if not, pour it into the pan with the chicken to heat up, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add contents of the pan into the pot and toss it together.
  8. Plate and serve!
