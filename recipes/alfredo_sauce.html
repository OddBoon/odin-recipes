Alfredo Sauce

Provided by Vanessa Greaves

Ingredients

  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese(or half Parmesan and half Romano)
  • ¼ cup butter
  • Salt/Pepper to taste
  • A pinch of Nutmeg

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan or skillet over med-low heat. Add to the butter garlic and cook for 1 min. Do not let it brown.
  2. Whisk in heavy cream and simmer gently for 5 mins to cook out the water, or reduce, the cream.
  3. Remove pan from heat and add grated cheese a handful at a time, whisking each addition until the cheese has melted.
  4. Return the pan to heat to simmer for 4-5 minutes, or until the sauce thickens. Do not let the sauce boil or eise it will searate and lose its smooth texture. Season with salt/pepper and add a pinch of nutmeg, if you so wish.
  5. Ready to serve or store!
